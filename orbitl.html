Orbit•L

ABOUT

Orbit•L was designed and engineered to solve the issue of being unable to upload your photos taken with your DSLR camera over WiFi. Unfortunately, due to an NDA with Canon Canada, I cannot disclose further details on how this prototype functions. However my role as the Design Lead involved flowcharting the process of this prototype, rendering the look and feel via Cinema4D, and presenting the final renders to Canon Canada.
