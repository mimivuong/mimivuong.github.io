Orbit•L
Upload photos from your DSLR camera to mobile device over WiFi
–
Client: Canon Inc.
Role: Design Lead

Older DSLR camera models do not have WiFi capabilities. As a result, my team and I prototyped a solution to help people unload their photos from their DSLR cameras to their mobile device over WiFi that is being broadcasted by Orbit•L.

My role involved flowcharting the user experience of this prototype, designing the brand, and rendering the final look and feel of this hardware on Cinema4D in order to pitch the prototype to the executives at Canon Canada, USA, and Japan.
