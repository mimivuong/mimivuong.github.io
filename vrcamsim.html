VR Camera Simulator

ABOUT

Redefining how consumers test out Canon products. The VR Camera Simulator provides an inexpensive experience for people to try out various combinations of Canon equipment in order to determine what their next purchase should be. Users can adjust aperture, shutter speed, and ISO and take a photo in an exciting immersive environment. Available on the Oculus Store here.
