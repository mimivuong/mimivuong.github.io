ABOUT

Mimi Vuong is a Director and Designer who was born in Vietnam, but raised in Canada. Her work can be found within the realms of design, film, and technology.

She is currently freelancing full-time by working with clients from Toronto and Los Angeles. Previously, she was the Design Lead at Canon Canada’s Innovation Lab, overseeing projects and launching products that are used worldwide.

She received her Bachelor of Design with Honours Degree from the joint program between York University and Sheridan College. During her time in school, she was selected as a semi-finalist in the Print Communications category for the Adobe Design Achievement Awards.
