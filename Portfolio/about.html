Infatuated with the unknown.

Mimi Vuong is a Director and Designer who was born in Vietnam, but raised in Canada. Her work can be found within the realms of design, film, and technology.

Currently she is freelancing full-time by working with clients from Toronto and Los Angeles. Previously, she was the Design Lead at Canon Canada’s Innovation Lab, overseeing projects and launching products that are used worldwide.

She received her Bachelor of Design with Honours degree from the York University and Sheridan College joint program. During her time in school, she was selected as a semi-finalist in the Print Communications category for the Adobe Design Achievement Awards.
